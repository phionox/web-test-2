Watch Care & Maintenance

Daily Care

Your Kinetic Watch is designed for everyday wear, but proper care will ensure it lasts for years to come.

Cleaning

  • Wipe with a soft, lint-free cloth daily
  • Use mild soap and water for deeper cleaning
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive materials
  • Dry thoroughly after exposure to water

Water Resistance

  • Water resistant up to 50 meters (5 ATM)
  • Safe for swimming and showering
  • Not suitable for diving or high-pressure water
  • Rinse after saltwater exposure

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Avoid extreme temperatures
  • Keep away from magnets and electronics
  • Use provided storage case when not wearing

Kinetic Charging

  • Wear daily for optimal charging
  • Movement generates energy automatically
  • Full charge lasts up to 6 months
  • LED indicator shows charge level
Pro Tip: Your watch charges through natural arm movement. Regular wear during daily activities is the best way to keep it fully charged.

Kinetic Technology

Our proprietary kinetic charging system converts your natural movement into electrical energy, eliminating the need for regular charging.

How It Works

A precision oscillating weight rotates with your arm movement, spinning a micro-generator that charges the internal battery. This elegant system has been refined over decades of watchmaking excellence.

Energy Storage

Advanced lithium-ion battery stores energy efficiently. Full charge provides up to 6 months of standby time, with typical usage lasting 2-3 weeks between active wear.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does kinetic charging work? +
The kinetic charging system uses your natural arm movement to generate power. An oscillating weight inside the watch rotates as you move, spinning a micro-generator that charges the battery. Normal daily wear is sufficient to keep the watch fully charged.
How long does a full charge last? +
With regular wear, your watch will maintain a full charge indefinitely. If unworn, a full charge provides up to 6 months of power reserve in standby mode, or 2-3 weeks with active smart features enabled.
Is the watch waterproof? +
The watch is water resistant to 50 meters (5 ATM), making it suitable for swimming, showering, and everyday water exposure. However, it is not designed for diving or high-pressure water activities.
Can I replace the battery? +
The battery is designed to last for many years with normal use. When replacement is eventually needed, it must be performed by an authorized service center to maintain water resistance and warranty coverage.
What smartphones are compatible? +
The Kinetic Watch is compatible with iOS 13.0 or later and Android 8.0 or later. Our companion app is available for free download on both the App Store and Google Play Store.
How accurate is the heart rate monitor? +
Our optical heart rate sensor provides medical-grade accuracy for most users. For best results, ensure the watch is worn snugly against your skin, especially during exercise. Note that factors like tattoos, skin tone, and excessive movement may affect readings.
Can I swim with my watch? +
Yes, the watch is safe for swimming in pools and open water. It features swim tracking modes and is designed to withstand water pressure up to 50 meters. Be sure to rinse with fresh water after swimming in saltwater or chlorinated pools.
How do I update the watch software? +
Software updates are delivered automatically through the companion app when your watch is connected to your smartphone. You'll receive a notification when an update is available. Updates typically take 5-10 minutes and should be done when the battery is at least 50% charged.
